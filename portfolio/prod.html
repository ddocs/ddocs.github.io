User Manuals

Next Generation Digital Printing

Tools used: Microsoft Word, Snagit, Adobe Photoshop

This client had secured a significant contract and required end user manuals urgently to accompany the shipping of their product overseas. They could offer remote technical support but their client needed a world class user manual to accompany their state of the art digital printer.

On the initial visit, one of the team demonstrated their product to me. It was quite awesome. Mindful of the need for a speedy delivery, I took extensive notes of every step and asked lots of questions from the more general to the very specific. In addition to the photos I took, the team were able to supply me with further screenshots and diagrams a few days later.

I focused on the end users who would be engaging with this product, their level of knowledge and the chronological sequence of their interaction with the product. I created a task based manual designed for printing with plenty of graphics to minimise translation costs at a later date. I reviewed the final draft with a happy team before sending the final manuals well within the time limit.
